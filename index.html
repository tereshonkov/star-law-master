About Transaction Helpdesk Ltd

Transaction Helpdesk Ltd is a boutique firm focusing on disputes around online payments, investment platforms, forex/CFD brokers and crypto-related transactions. We work with clients who face problems with chargebacks, rejected withdrawals, account blocks and unclear contractual terms.

Our role is to analyse your situation from a legal and procedural perspective: we review contracts, correspondence and transaction history, explain the risks in plain language and outline realistic scenarios before you decide how to proceed. We do not promise “guaranteed recovery” — instead, we provide honest assessment and structured next steps.

How we work with payment and platform disputes

Our goal is to give you a realistic view of your position in disputes with banks, payment providers, brokers and crypto platforms, and to build a strategy that matches your objectives.

  • Review of contracts, platform terms, statements and transaction history.
  • Analysis of correspondence with the bank, broker or exchange.
  • Explanation of possible legal arguments and procedural options.
  • Preparation of letters, complaints or claims where appropriate.
  • Coordination with local counsel if the dispute involves multiple jurisdictions.
  • Regular updates so you understand the status of your matter.
  • No misleading promises — we do not guarantee recovery, but we work diligently on your case.
