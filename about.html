Transaction Helpdesk Ltd

Transaction Helpdesk Ltd is a boutique practice focused on disputes related to card payments, bank transfers, online brokers and digital asset platforms. Many of these matters are complex: several intermediaries may be involved, different contracts apply and communication is spread across emails, chats and account interfaces.

Our role is to help clients understand the legal and procedural framework around their situation. We look at how and when payments were made, which terms and conditions applied, how complaints were handled and what regulatory or scheme rules may be relevant.

We do not offer investment services and do not provide financial or tax advice. Instead, we focus on legal analysis and documentation: what has been agreed in writing, how the provider acted and which steps may be available under applicable procedures.

Every case is different. Some matters may have a higher chance of escalation through banks, card schemes or other channels, while others may be limited by timeframes, missing documentation or contractual restrictions. We explain these factors in clear language so that clients can make informed decisions about possible next steps.

Nothing on this page or elsewhere on the website should be understood as a promise of success or a guarantee of any financial result. Outcomes in legal and procedural matters depend on the facts of each case and on decisions taken by third parties such as banks, payment institutions and authorities.
